Basic R courses for FOOD UCPH

This site distributes content from short intensive R-courses at Deparment of Food Science (University of Copenhagen). At the moment and basic introduction (BASICs) with plotting using ggplot2 and simple statistics, and a Reproducible data analysis with Rmarkdown (RePro) have been completed. Feel free to use the content on this side including data sets.

For full details see the /github including /data, Notes for Data Analysis in Food Science, more data for the notes and some /videos.

Happy coding.
